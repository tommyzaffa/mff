Masterclass — Ethics & Authorship

  • Type Masterclass
  • Date 12 Sep
  • Time 15:30

Description

A deep-dive masterclass on ethical questions and authorship in AI-generated cinema. This session explores responsibility, creative ownership, training data, and the evolving role of the human author in machine-assisted filmmaking.
