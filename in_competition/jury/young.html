Youth Jury

The Merge Film Festival’s Youth Jury is designed to offer young people, students and film enthusiasts an experience at the heart of the festival.

Selected participants will be actively involved throughout the four-day event, with access to all screenings and the opportunity to view the films being shown. In particular, attendance at the six screenings dedicated to the official competition (between Friday and Saturday) — the central part of the jury’s programme — will be required. At the same time, participants will take part in discussions and analysis sessions in dedicated spaces, accompanied by an industry professional, with the aim of developing a critical eye and sharing reflections on contemporary cinematic languages. The work of the Youth Jury will also lead to the awarding of a dedicated prize.

Participants will receive the following free of charge:
– a weekly pass granting access to screenings;
– participation in meetings and discussions with professionals, exclusively for the youth jury;
– a formative experience within the festival, in close contact with the programme and its protagonists/guests.

To apply, please send an email including: a brief personal introduction, a cover letter and an analysis of a film you have recently seen. Selected candidates will subsequently be contacted by email.
