About us

PoPaCi Pictures is an audiovisual production company based in Ticino, specialising in the development of film projects and initiatives that explore the relationship between image, technology and contemporary society. Its work is situated within a field of research that spans visual languages and the transformations brought about by emerging technologies, with a particular focus on the cultural and perceptual implications of artificial intelligence.

The Merge Film Festival was conceived as a space for encounter and dialogue, becoming the natural extension of the PoPaCi project across the canton. The festival serves as a cultural platform where cinema and artificial intelligence are brought together to challenge the viewer to reflect on creative processes and on the ways in which these technologies redefine the imagination and the experience of reality.

Organising team

Dario Cirrincione

General Manager & Artistic Director

A Swiss director, writer, audiovisual programmer and producer, Dario Cirrincione serves as General Manager and Artistic Director of the Merge Film Festival. He is co-founder of the independent company PoPaCi Pictures SNC, through which he develops film and art projects as well as cultural initiatives in Switzerland and internationally. He directed Present, a short film that explores and blends various expressive formats, including the use of artificial intelligence. The film has been selected for several international festivals, including the World AI Film Festival.

Francesco Poloni

Chief Operating Officer & Artistic Director

A Swiss director, writer, audiovisual programmer and producer, Francesco Poloni serves as Chief Operating Officer and Artistic Director of the Merge Film Festival. He is co-founder of the independent company PoPaCi Pictures SNC, through which he develops film and art projects and cultural initiatives between Switzerland and the international scene. He directed the short film Tusen Toner, which was selected for the Pardi di domani section of the 2025 Locarno Film Festival, where it won the SRG SSR Silver Pardino, one of the leading awards dedicated to emerging filmmakers.

Liam Pagnamenta

Financial Director & Artistic Director

Swiss producer and audiovisual programmer Liam Pagnamenta serves as Financial Director and Artistic Director of the Merge Film Festival. He is co-founder of the independent company PoPaCi Pictures SNC, through which he develops film and artistic projects and cultural initiatives in Switzerland and internationally. He is currently producing a short film created entirely using artificial intelligence, which explores AI and its impact on the human condition, examining how it redefines perception, identity and our relationship with reality.
